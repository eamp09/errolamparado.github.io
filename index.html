Career Journey

I began my career in 2016 as a Sales, Billing, and Technical Support Representative for Convergys PH

I handled U.S.-based accounts like Comcast and Spectrum providing billing, technical and customer support.

In this role, I developed a solid foundation in customer-centric operations, strengthening my communication, problem-solving, and technical troubleshooting skills. I handled internet connectivity issues, billing concerns, and service inquiries while consistently meeting performance KPIs and maintaining high customer satisfaction. This experience enhanced my ability to analyze issues quickly, manage high workloads, and deliver clear, effective solutions. I also gained exposure to sales by identifying customer needs and supporting upselling opportunities and skills that later supported my transition into e-commerce and marketplace operations.

In 2018, I worked as an Inventory Controller at MUMUSO General Trading LLC

Managed inventory operations, supported marketing and data analysis, and gained hands-on exposure to e-commerce marketplace management.

This experience allowed me to understand the full lifecycle of digital sales channels, from stock forecasting and procurement to performance monitoring and operational optimization. This role strengthened my expertise in inventory forecasting, demand planning, supply chain coordination, and product management. I also developed skills in cross-functional collaboration, working closely with warehouse, logistics, and finance teams to ensure timely deliveries, accurate invoicing, and minimal stock discrepancies.

In 2022 I joined Geekay Distribution FZCO as an E-Commerce Marketplace Specialist

I now manage marketplace and quick-commerce operations across multiple GCC platforms.

I oversee operations on Amazon, Noon, Talabat, Instashop, and other GCC marketplaces, focusing on analytics-driven decision-making, inventory optimization, product visibility, and marketplace compliance. I manage product listings, pricing, and promotions while leading initiatives in operational automation, KPI tracking, and campaign performance to drive sales growth and improve overall marketplace efficiency.
