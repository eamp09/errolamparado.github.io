About Me

I am an experienced E-Commerce Marketplace Specialist with over seven years of progressive experience managing operations across Amazon, Noon, Carrefour, Talabat, Instashop, Namshi, Trendyol, and other GCC platforms. My career journey reflects a continuous evolution from customer support to inventory management, and now to full-scale e-commerce marketplace operations.

In 2016, I began as a Billing & Technical Support Representative at Convergys PH, supporting U.S.-based accounts such as Comcast and Spectrum, developing a strong foundation in problem-solving, process management, and customer service excellence.

From 2018 to 2022, I worked as an Inventory Controller at MUMUSO General Trading LLC, UAE, overseeing stock ordering, replenishment, and warehouse coordination. During this time, I gained exposure to e-commerce operations, which sparked my passion for platform management, product listings, and market analysis.

Since 2022, I have been an E-Commerce Marketplace Specialist at Geekay Distribution FZCO, UAE, managing end-to-end operations, including order processing, inventory accuracy, product listing optimization, pricing strategy, campaign execution, and customer support. This role allows me to combine operational expertise with analytical insights to drive business growth and marketplace success.

I am naturally curious and eager to learn, constantly seeking new knowledge and skills that enhance both my career and personal growth. This mindset drives me to adapt quickly, embrace challenges, and continually improve in the ever-evolving world of e-commerce.
