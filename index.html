I handled internet connectivity issues, billing concerns, and service inquiries while consistently meeting performance KPIs and maintaining high customer satisfaction. This experience enhanced my ability to analyze issues quickly, manage high workloads, and deliver clear, effective solutions. I also gained exposure to sales by identifying customer needs and supporting upselling opportunities and skills that later supported my transition into e-commerce and marketplace operations.

In 2018, I worked as an Inventory Controller at MUMUSO General Trading LLC

During the pandemic I was exposed to e-commerce operations including marketplace management.

This experience allowed me to understand the full lifecycle of digital sales channels, from stock forecasting and procurement to performance monitoring and operational optimization. This role strengthened my expertise in inventory forecasting, demand planning, supply chain coordination, and product management. I also developed skills in cross-functional collaboration, working closely with warehouse, logistics, and finance teams to ensure timely deliveries, accurate invoicing, and minimal stock discrepancies. These experiences laid the foundation for my transition into full-time e-commerce and marketplace operations, where I now leverage data-driven insights, optimize product listings for SEO and conversions, manage promotional campaigns, and streamline workflows to maximize sales, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

In 2022 I joined Geekay Distribution FZCO as an E-Commerce Marketplace Specialist

I now manage marketplace and quick-commerce operations across multiple GCC platforms.

I oversee operations on Amazon, Noon, Talabat, Instashop, and other GCC marketplaces, focusing on analytics-driven decision-making, inventory optimization, product visibility, and marketplace compliance. I manage product listings, pricing, and promotions while leading initiatives in operational automation, KPI tracking, and campaign performance to drive sales growth and improve overall marketplace efficiency. I also design and maintain dashboards for sales, inventory, and campaign ROI, enabling proactive decision-making and rapid response to market trends. Collaborating closely with marketing, logistics, and finance teams, I optimize inventory turnover, reduce stockouts, and maximize profitability, applying strong skills in data analysis, process improvement, cross-functional coordination, and strategic planning to support business growth.
